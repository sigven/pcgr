# Primary Tumor Sites

PCGR integrates a variety of different datasets relating to precision
cancer medicine, that often use different ontologies when it comes to
diseases or phenotypes. We have cross-referenced a variety of ontologies
(DiseaseOntology, EFO, OncoTree, UMLS, ICD-10) that are in use in the
oncology domain, and organized all the various tumor subtypes into 30
different main tumor sites (see also
<https://github.com/sigven/phenOncoX>).

Below you can browse all the various phenotype terms (including specific
tumor subtypes) that are associated with each of the primary sites. This
as a guide to choose the primary tumor type when running PCGR for a
given sample (option `--tumor_site`).
